Different Types of Travel Websites
There are a variety of different types of travel websites available, including:
Inbound and outbound tours: offered by travel agents, these tours help visitors plan their trip before they go.
Tourism: A website helps tourists find things to do and places to see while on vacation specific to a particular location.
Travel booking sites: these websites help you find a place to stay while on vacation.
Travel agency website: A travel agency provides services to their target audience, which includes being a travel planner, working with tour operators, and providing booking advice and deals.
Activity websites: these websites help you find things to do while you are on vacation.
Vacation package websites: these websites help you find a package deal for your vacation.
